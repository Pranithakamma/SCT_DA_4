#SkillCraft Technology Internship - Task 04

##Project Title:
Business Insights Report using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

---

##Project Overview

This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a marketing campaign dataset to extract meaningful business insights. The goal is to analyze campaign performance across different marketing channels and provide data-driven recommendations.

---

##Objectives

- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing
- Analyze marketing campaign performance
- Understand customer engagement patterns
- Compare different marketing channels
- Identify high-performing strategies using ROI
- Provide business recommendations

---

##Tools & Technologies Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Google Colab / Jupyter Notebook

---

##Dataset Description

The dataset contains information about marketing campaigns, including:

- Campaign_ID
- Campaign_Type
- Target_Audience
- Channel_Used
- Duration
- Clicks
- Impressions
- Engagement_Score
- ROI
- Customer_Segment
- Location
- Language

---

##Key Analysis Performed

- Data Cleaning (handling missing values and duplicates)
- Analysis of marketing channels based on:
  - ROI (Return on Investment)
  - Clicks
  - Impressions
- Relationship analysis between:
  - Impressions and Clicks
  - Engagement Score and Channel
- Customer segment analysis
- Campaign performance comparison

---

##Key Insights

- Marketing channels show different performance levels in terms of ROI and engagement.
- Higher impressions generally lead to higher clicks, indicating effective reach.
- Some channels perform better in ROI, making them more cost-effective.
- Customer segments respond differently to marketing campaigns.

---

##Business Recommendations

- Focus more budget on high ROI marketing channels
- Improve strategies for low-performing campaigns
- Target specific customer segments for better engagement
- Optimize campaigns to increase conversions and efficiency

---

##Conclusion

This project demonstrates how Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) helps in understanding marketing performance and making data-driven business decisions. By analyzing campaign data, businesses can improve their strategies and maximize return on investment.
